Older School Buildings are More Likely to Carry a Higher Seismic Risk

Comparing the year of construction with the risk level for 303 buildings in which information was available showed that older buildings tend to have higher seismic risk levels than buildings constructed more recently. This sample represents 55 percent of the school buildings assessed for the SSSP. These buildings were assessed from 2019-2021, as part of Phase 2 of the SSSP.
